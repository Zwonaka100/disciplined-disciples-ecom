Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Disciplined Disciples.

What materials are your products made from?

Our apparel is crafted from premium, high-quality fabrics, primarily focusing on soft-touch cotton blends and durable fleeces for comfort and longevity. Specific material details are available on each product's detail page.

How do I choose the right size?

We provide a detailed size guide on each product page. We recommend comparing your measurements to our guide to find the perfect fit. If you're between sizes, consider your preferred fit (snug vs. relaxed).

What is your shipping policy?

We offer nationwide shipping within South Africa. Orders are typically processed within 2-3 business days. Shipping times and costs vary based on your location and chosen shipping method. Please see our dedicated Shipping & Returns page for more details.

Can I return or exchange an item?

Yes, we accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of purchase, provided the item is unworn, unwashed, and in its original condition with tags attached. Custom or personalized items may not be eligible. For full details, please visit our Shipping & Returns policy.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to the courier's website. You can use this to monitor the status of your delivery.
