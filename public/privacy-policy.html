Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us.

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Disciplined Disciples collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you visit or make a purchase from our website. This policy complies with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

POPIA Compliance

Responsible Party: Disciplined Disciples

Information Officer: Zande Technologies (Pty) Ltd

Information Regulator Registration Number: 2025-066656

Organisation Type: Private Organisation

Contact: zmabege@gmail.com

Information We Collect

When you visit the Site, we collect certain information about your device, your interaction with the Site, and information necessary to process your purchases. We may also collect additional information if you contact us for customer support.

Personal Information Examples:

  • Name and surname
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Billing address
  • Shipping address
  • Payment information (processed securely by PayFast)
  • Order history and purchase details
  • Device information and IP address
  • Cookies and browsing behavior (with your consent)

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to provide our services to you, which includes:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders
  • Processing payments through our secure payment provider (PayFast)
  • Communicating with you about your orders
  • Sending order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Providing customer support
  • Improving our website and services
  • Sending marketing communications (only with your consent)
  • Complying with legal obligations

Legal Basis for Processing

Under POPIA, we process your personal information based on:

  • Your consent: When you sign up, place orders, or accept cookies
  • Contractual necessity: To fulfill orders and provide services
  • Legal compliance: To comply with tax, accounting, and legal requirements
  • Legitimate interests: To improve our services and prevent fraud

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in to your account
  • Remember your cart items
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior (Google Analytics)
  • Improve your browsing experience

You can accept or decline cookies through our cookie consent banner. Declining analytics cookies will disable Google Analytics tracking while preserving essential website functionality.

Sharing Your Personal Information

We share your Personal Information with trusted service providers who help us operate our business:

  • Zande Technologies (Pty) Ltd: As the registered Information Officer (Reg: 2025-066656) and platform developer, Zande Technologies has administrative access to all data stored in the system, including customer information, orders, and analytics. This access is necessary for platform maintenance, technical support, security monitoring, and compliance purposes.
  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and hosting services
  • PayFast: Secure payment processing (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Google Analytics: Website analytics (only with your consent)
  • Email service providers: To send order confirmations and updates
  • Courier services: To deliver your orders

Important: Zande Technologies, as the Information Officer, maintains technical and administrative control over the platform and has the ability to access, modify, and manage all data within the system for operational, security, and compliance purposes.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may also share your information to comply with applicable laws, respond to legal requests, or protect our rights.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Account data: Until you request deletion or 2 years after last activity
  • Order history: 5 years (for tax and legal compliance)
  • Marketing communications: Until you unsubscribe
  • Cookies: Up to 2 years or until you clear them

Your Rights Under POPIA

As a South African resident, you have the following rights:

1. Right to Access

Request a copy of your personal information we hold

2. Right to Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data

3. Right to Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations)

4. Right to Object

Object to processing of your data for marketing purposes

5. Right to Data Portability

Request a copy of your data in a structured format

6. Right to Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

7. Right to Lodge a Complaint

Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. These measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure Firebase authentication and database rules
  • PCI DSS compliant payment processing through PayFast
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls limiting who can view your data

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
